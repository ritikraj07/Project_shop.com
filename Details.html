FAQ

How do I use the Motives Gel-ous Brow Gel?

Using the provided applicator, apply directly onto brows in short, upward strokes to shape, fill and style the brows.

Pro Tips:

  • Create volume with a few coats of short, upward strokes.
  • For a more natural-looking brow, start your strokes at the inner brow and move outward.
  • For full-on power brows, use short strokes starting against the hair growth direction and start at the tail of the brow. Allow time for the gel to dry, and then brush hairs back in place.
  • Use the tip of the applicator for the arch and tail of the brow to define and shape.
  • Use the Motives Arch Definer Ultra-Fine Brow Pencil to add hair-like strokes to fill empty spots and areas


What if I just want to tame and shape the brows I have and not add color?

We offer a "clear" version of the Motive Gel-ous Brow Gel that will help tame, shape and fluff brows without adding additional color.


Are there any other products I could use in addition to the Motives Gel-ous Brow Gel?

We recommend pairing the brow gel with our Motives Arch Definer Ultra Fine Brow Pencil and/or our Motives Essential Brow Kit. Use whatever combination of products you would like to help you feel confident in your brows.


Is the Motives Gel-ous Brow Gel waterproof?

While our Motives Gel-ous Brow Gel is not waterproof, it is formulated to stick with you all day!


Is Motives Gel-ous Brow Gel tested on animals?

Motives does not conduct or commission animal testing of any finished product.
